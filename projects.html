ETF Meter

This project is pursued with the intent of exploring daily stock data and rewarding long term investing strategies. I'm aiming to provide analytics for long-term investors or retirees looking for a larger return, while minimizing risk. The app estimates gain, gap, and volatility of stocks using statistical and machine learning methods and outputs a heatmap of scores* for a specified horizon.

My plans are to develop an optimum allocation module and inject a recurrent deep learning module to get more reliable predictions.

The repository is currently private, but feel free to reach out if you're interested!
